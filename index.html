Hobbies

One thing about me is that I LOVE to cook! One of my favorite recipes to make is Egg Fried Rice. The recipe is as follows:

Ingredients
  • Cooked Rice
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Corn
  • Pepper, Soysauce
Instructions
  1. Prepare all the ingredients and dice vegetables
  2. Heat up a pan to medium heat with oil
  3. Stir fry the eggs and remove from pan
  4. Stir fry the diced vegetables
  5. Add back in the eggs and rice
  6. Add rest of spices and stir fry for 5 more minutes
  7. Remove from stove and serve!
